About

We thought Lumen was a failure, and hoped to create an editorial which Josephians would actually reckon with. We hope to create a platform where not only we the creaters, but also other Josephians can actively submit pieces to us. Teachers' voices would be a bonus. We want to document accounts of school events, encourage debate on any issue between students and be the first source of news and shared experience in SJI. We see ourselves as a connecting force between Josephians, binding us not just in a common campus, but in spirit too, even outside the gates of SJI.
